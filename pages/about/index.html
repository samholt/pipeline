New discoveries require new ways of thinking

New discoveries often require new ways of thinking to grow and flourish -- new notations, visualizations, or mental models. These mental user interfaces unlock our ability to truly engage with these ideas. Sadly, this is rarely even acknowledged. By nurturing the human dimension of research, Distill hopes to make ideas more approachable and to create fertile ground for making new discoveries.

An expressive medium for expressing ideas

The modern Web gives us powerful tools to share new ways of thinking -- to create user interfaces for our ideas. Over the last few years we've seen many incredible demonstrations of this kind of work. PDFs are so last century.

Lending a helping hand

Many researchers want to do this kind of work, but can’t get the support they need to pursue it. We, as the research community, are failing them. We don’t treat their wonderful, non-traditional research artifacts as “real” academic contributions.

Why machine learning?

Machine Learning is the biggest UI challenge -- and opportunity -- of the decade. The biggest advances in UI will be driven by problems challenges in Machine Learning. Conversely, deeply understanding machine learning, and solving problems like transparency, is deeply connected to user interface design.
