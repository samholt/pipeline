New discoveries require new ways of thinking

New discoveries often require new ways of thinking to grow and flourish -- new notations, visualizations, or mental models. These mental user interfaces unlock our ability to truly engage with these ideas. Sadly, this is rarely acknowledged, let alone nurtured. By embracing this human dimension of research, Distill hopes to create fertile ground for making new discoveries.

A modern medium for presenting research

The modern Web gives us powerful tools to share new ways of thinking -- to create user interfaces for our ideas. Over the last few years we've seen many incredible demonstrations of this kind of work. PDFs are so last century.

How will machine learning benefit from Distill’s approach?

Machine learning will fundamentally change how humans and computers interact. It’s important to make those techniques transparent, so we can understand and safely control how they work. Distill will provide a platform for vividly illustrating these ideas.

Legitimizing non-traditional research artifiacts

Many researchers want to do this kind of work, but can’t get the support they need to pursue it. We, as the research community, are failing them. We don’t treat their wonderful, non-traditional research artifacts as “real” academic contributions.
