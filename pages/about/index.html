A modern medium for presenting research

The web is a powerful medium to share new ways of thinking. Over the last few years we’ve seen many imaginative examples of such work. But traditional academic publishing remains focused on the PDF. Distill will embrace modern media.

New ways of thinking enable new discoveries

New notations, visualizations, and mental models can deepen our understanding. By nurturing the development of such new ways of thinking, Distill will enable new discoveries.

Machine Learning needs more transparency

Machine learning will fundamentally change how humans and computers interact. It’s important to make those techniques transparent, so we can understand and safely control how they work. Distill will provide a platform for vividly illustrating these ideas.

Legitimizing non-traditional research artifacts

Many researchers want to do this kind of work, but can’t get the support they need to pursue it. We, as the research community, are failing them. We don’t treat their wonderful, non-traditional research artifacts as “real” academic contributions.

Taking the time to explain things well benefits the community

As we rush papers out the door to meet conference deadlines without taking the time to really refine and edit them well for our audience, we are piling onto the amount of work our audience has to do to keep current in the field. This piling up of “research debt” is a drag on the community. We believe that doing the hard work of refining and editing before publishing will have a huge impact on the work we do as a community to understand each others results.
